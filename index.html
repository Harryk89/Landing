Profile S300

Profile S300 suits for solving simple and complex problems in architecture and construction. Lightweight and durable profile has good performance characteristics, guarantees protection against increased temperature. The reasonable price of the profile system S300 will make it possible to implement large-scale projects and to glaze any object in a profitable way.

  • Number of chambers:  3

  • Profile width:  58мм

  • Frame height:  62мм

  • Glazed unit width:  24мм, 33мм

  • Outer wall thickness:  2,6мм

  • Soundproofing:  VDI 2719

  • Noise insulation class:  II до IV

  • Thermal transmittance:  R=0.63 m2K/Bт

Multifuncional glass Double Silver Steko

Multifuncional glass Double Silver Steko is used for different purposes simultaneously. Thanks to special silver ions this glass:

  • - protects from harmful ultraviolet radiation

  • - reflects heat energy, preventing overheating of the room

  • - keeps coolness indoors

  • - guarantees light transmission over 72%

  • - provides a comfortable microclimate

  • - eliminates surfaces’ discoloration

  • - improves the look of the facade


The multifunctional glass Double Silver Steko is installed from the outside by its coating inside the glass unit. It is also used for any type of glazing.
